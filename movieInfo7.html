ABORTION-STORIES-WOMEN-TELL

by Tracy Droz Tragos

Abortion: Stories Women Tell is a 2016 American documentary film directed and produced by Tracy Droz Tragos

The film centers on different women on either side of the abortion debate in the state of Missouri. It had its premiere at the 2016 Tribeca Film Festival on April 18.
