Leave No Trace

by Ian McEwan

Leave No Trace is a 2018 American drama film directed by Debra Granik and written by Granik and Anne Rosellini, based on the 2009 novel My Abandonment

A father and his daughter lead a peaceful life in the woods in Portland, Oregon. However, their fate changes after an incident derail their lives. Initial release: 27 April 2018 (USA) Director: Debra Granik Based on: My Abandonment; by Peter Rock Produced by: Anne Harrison; Linda Reisman; Anne Rosellini Box office: $7.7 million Music by: Dickon Hinchliffe
